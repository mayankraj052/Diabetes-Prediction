Frequently Asked Questions

Got any doubts? May be this can help you!

What is Polyuria?

Polyuria means Frequent Urination.

Who is Polydipsia?

Polydipsia is a condition where a person feels thirst.

What is Polyphagia?

Excessive feeling of hunger.

Waht is Genital thrush?

Genital thrush is a a yeast infection in the genital area that is more likely to occur in people with diabetes due to their high blood sugar levels.

Waht is Alopecia?

Alopecia is a condition that causes hair loss from some or all areas of the body.

What is Partial Paresis?

Paresis involves the weakening of a muscle or group of muscles. It may also be referred to as partial or mild paralysis..
